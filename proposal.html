What I see costing you commercial leads right now.

From a first look at your website, your tools, and the GTA market, here's where the leaks are. None of this is a criticism — it's exactly what a new startup looks like before the engine is built. It's also the fastest ROI.

You already pay for GoHighLevel, Zoho CRM, and Lemlist — but there's no live outbound to facility managers, no instant speed-to-lead follow-up, and no automated nurture. You're paying for the engine and driving it by hand.
